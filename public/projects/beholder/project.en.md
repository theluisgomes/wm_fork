Beholder is a geopolitical intelligence platform built by Wisemetrics to make armed conflicts around the world understandable. Through an interactive map, users can explore the scale of each war, the countries involved, estimated costs, casualty numbers, and multiple perspectives on each situation. The platform includes a humanitarian layer focused on civilian victims and a comparative view across conflicts. The goal is to turn complexity into clarity, for anyone who needs to understand the world as it really is.
